Who am I?



Hi, I'm Jon. I'm a developer, electrical engineer, challenge seeker, opportunity finder, and soon to be a world changer.


I was born and raised in NJ and am now a recent transplant in beautiful Miami Florida. I came here to pursue my true passion, programming. I coding 'bug' bit me back in highschool. I spent WAY too many math classes concentrating on writing fun apps on my Ti-83+ calculator. I went from making basic quadratic formula apps, to snake type games, to instant messaging apps and never had so much fun in class.


I orginally started college pursuing a Computer Engineering degree. I took a C++ course freshmen year and loved it. Next up was an accelerated Java I/II course. This is the course that made me think I couldn't make it as a programmer. The problem was that I was expected to alredy understand OOP before starting the class. I had never even heard of the OOP and thus became hopelessly lost right away. It was this Java course that lead me to switch to an Electrical Engineering degree.


Fast forward several years of working as an Electrical Engineer and I wasn't happy. I knew what I wanted to do. I want to code. I worked on some side projects in my limited free time. I built a basic Android app and built a Tip Calculator app for iOS. The coding 'bug' bit again. This time even stronger than before. I decided to trade in the stability of a good job that I didn't enjoy for the uncertainty of a brand new career. I left NJ, drove down to Miami, and started Wyncode Cohort 6!
